MMD Generator

Work in progress!

If you know MBTI and cognitive functions and you want to get a visual chart of your personal function, you came in the right place! MMD (Mind Muscle Diagram) is a diagram which allows you to see graphically how much you have developped your cognitive functions. If you want to learn more about MMD, check this out. Now, if you want to use this website, you first need to take this MBTI Cognitive Functions test (I think it is made by Dario Nardi). Once you know the points of your functions, fill the form with your data. And also insert your MBTI type. Than press the "generate MMD" button. Enjoy!
